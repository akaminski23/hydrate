# Sip - Hydration Tracker Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I log a drink?**
Tap the "Drink" button on the Home screen to quickly add 250ml of water. For other drinks (coffee, tea, juice), tap the water droplet icon to select drink type and amount.

**How do I set my daily goal?**
Go to Settings > Daily Goal. You can set a goal between 2000-4000ml based on your hydration needs.

**How do I change units to oz?**
Go to Settings > Units and select "oz" for fluid ounces.

### Reminders

**How do I set up reminders?**
Go to Settings > Reminders and toggle them on. You can customize:
- Start time (when reminders begin)
- End time (when reminders stop)
- Frequency (every 1, 2, 3, or 4 hours)

**I'm not receiving notifications**
1. Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Sip
2. Ensure reminders are toggled on in the app Settings
3. Make sure Do Not Disturb is not active

### History & Data

**How do I delete a drink entry?**
Go to the History tab and swipe left on any entry to delete it.

**How do I reset today's progress?**
Go to Settings and tap "Reset Today's Progress" at the bottom.

**Is my data backed up?**
- Free users: Data is stored locally on your device
- Sip Pro: iCloud sync keeps your data safe across devices

### Sip Pro

**What's included in Sip Pro?**
- Advanced analytics with weekly/monthly trends
- Smart AI-powered reminders
- Unlimited custom drinks
- iCloud sync across devices
- Custom themes and icons

**How do I restore my purchase?**
On the Pro upgrade screen, tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Find Sip and tap Cancel

### Troubleshooting

**The app is crashing**
Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Delete and reinstall (note: this clears local data)

**My progress isn't syncing (Pro)**
1. Ensure you're signed into iCloud
2. Check that iCloud Drive is enabled for Sip
3. Wait a few minutes and try again
