## Description (EN)
Stay healthy and hydrated with Hydrate - your personal water tracking companion!

Track your daily water intake with a beautiful, easy-to-use interface. Set personalized hydration goals and receive gentle reminders throughout the day to keep you on track.

FEATURES:
- Beautiful circular progress gauge
- Customizable daily goals (2000-4000 mL)
- Smart reminders at your preferred intervals
- Complete hydration history
- Support for mL and oz units
- Clean, intuitive design

Whether you're at work, exercising, or relaxing at home, Hydrate helps you maintain optimal hydration for better health and energy levels.

Download now and start your journey to better hydration!
